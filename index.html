Who Are We?

Welcome to DoBu Martial Arts, your premier destination for martial arts training, fitness, and self-defense courses. At DoBu, we are dedicated to empowering individuals of all ages and backgrounds to unlock their true potential through the practice of martial arts and the pursuit of a healthy lifestyle.

Mission

DoBu Martial Arts envisions a world where individuals of all ages and backgrounds are empowered through the pursuit of martial arts excellence. We strive to inspire confidence, discipline, and respect in our students, equipping them with invaluable life skills that extend far beyond the dojo. Our vision is to create martial artists who lead with integrity, contribute positively to their communities, and face life's challenges with unwavering determination.

Vision

At DoBu Martial Arts, our vision is to create a transformative and empowering experience for individuals through the pursuit of martial arts excellence. We believe that martial arts is not just a physical practice; it is a way of life that instills valuable life skills and nurtures personal growth. Our vision extends far beyond simply teaching martial arts techniques; we aim to inspire confidence, discipline, and respect in our students, empowering them to become leaders both on and off the training mat.

World-class instructors

At DoBu Martial Arts, our team of skilled and experienced instructors, led by gym owner Mauricio Gomez, offers expertise in judo, jiu-jitsu, karate, and Muay Thai. With a passion for teaching, they empower students to grow physically and mentally through their martial arts journey.
