Our Instructors

Mauricio Gomez

(Gym Owner/ Head Martial Arts Coach)

Mauricio Gomez, the Gym Owner and Head Martial Arts Coach at DoBu Martial Arts, is a highly respected figure in the martial arts world. With a 4th Dan Blackbelt in Judo, 3rd Dan Blackbelt in Jiu-Jitsu, and 1st Dan Blackbelt in Karate, he excels in all disciplines. An Accredited Muay Thai coach, Mauricio fosters discipline and respect among students, creating a supportive learning environment.
